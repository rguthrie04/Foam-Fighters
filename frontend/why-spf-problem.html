The Critical Problems with Spray Foam Insulation

Structural Damage & Building Issues

  • Prevents Roof Inspection: Makes it impossible to inspect roof timbers, leading to hidden rot and structural issues
  • Moisture Trapping: Can trap moisture against timber, accelerating decay and causing serious structural problems
  • Ventilation Blocking: Blocks essential roof ventilation, leading to condensation and damp issues
  • Removal Damage: Often damages roof timbers during removal, requiring expensive repairs

Mortgage Application Failures

  • Automatic Refusals: Many major lenders now automatically refuse mortgages on properties with spray foam
  • Survey Red Flags: Building surveyors flag spray foam as a major concern in mortgage valuations
  • Limited Lender Options: Drastically reduces the number of available mortgage products
  • Higher Rates: Lenders that do accept often charge significantly higher interest rates

Insurance Coverage Issues

  • Policy Refusals: Insurance companies increasingly refuse to cover properties with spray foam
  • Policy Cancellations: Existing policies may be cancelled when spray foam is discovered
  • Coverage Exclusions: Specific exclusions for damage related to spray foam insulation
  • Premium Increases: Significant premium increases when coverage is available

Property Sale Complications

  • Reduced Property Value: Properties with spray foam typically sell for significantly less
  • Buyer Withdrawal: Many potential buyers withdraw offers when spray foam is discovered
  • Extended Marketing: Properties stay on the market much longer than comparable homes
  • Chain Collapse: Mortgage issues can cause entire property chains to collapse

Industry Response to Spray Foam Problems

Major Lenders

  • Halifax/Lloyds: Refuses all spray foam properties
  • Nationwide: Case-by-case basis with strict criteria
  • Santander: Requires specialist surveyor reports
  • Barclays: Significant lending restrictions

Insurance Companies

  • Aviva: Specific spray foam exclusions
  • Direct Line: Refuses new policies
  • LV=: Premium loading for spray foam properties
  • Admiral: Case-by-case assessment required
