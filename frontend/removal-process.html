Post-Removal: What to Expect

Important Understanding

Spray foam removal does not restore the property to its original pre-foam condition. The goal is to remove the barrier to mortgage approval and property sale.

What's Achieved

  • Complete foam removal
  • Roof timber inspection access restored
  • Ventilation pathways reopened
  • Mortgage barriers removed
  • Insurance coverage restored
  • Property saleability restored
  • Professional certification provided

Realistic Expectations

  • Some surface marking may remain
  • Timber may show removal evidence
  • Energy efficiency will be reduced
  • Alternative insulation needed
  • Some repair work may be required
  • Property won't return to pre-foam state

Alternative Insulation Options
