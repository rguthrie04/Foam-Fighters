Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Foam Fighters Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Contact Details:

  • Company: Foam Fighters Ltd

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:
  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number
  • Property Information: Address, property type, foam details
  • Service Details: Urgency level, project requirements, additional details
  • Communication: Messages, calls, and service interactions
Automatically Collected Information:
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, referral sources
  • Analytics: Website performance and user behavior data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide quotes, assessments, and spray foam removal services
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries and keep you updated on your project
  • Business Operations: To manage bookings, scheduling, and service delivery
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory requirements and industry standards
  • Improvement: To enhance our services and website functionality
  • Marketing: To send relevant information about our services (with your consent)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: When you provide explicit consent for marketing communications
  • Contract: To fulfill our service agreements with you
  • Legitimate Interest: For business operations, customer service, and website improvement
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with health and safety regulations

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Partners: Trusted contractors and suppliers involved in your project
  • Professional Advisors: Lawyers, accountants, and insurance providers
  • Regulatory Bodies: When required by law or regulation
  • Technology Providers: Website hosting, analytics, and communication services

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Secure website hosting with SSL encryption
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Staff training on data protection principles
  • Secure disposal of data when no longer needed

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain customer relationships
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Meet insurance and warranty obligations

Typically, we retain customer data for 7 years after the completion of services, unless a longer period is required by law.

8. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Restriction: Ask us to limit processing of your data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@foamfighters.co.uk or 0333 577 0132.

9. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Ensure proper website functionality
  • Analyze website usage and performance
  • Improve user experience
  • Remember your preferences

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

10. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services including:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Firebase: For website hosting and form processing
  • Bootstrap/CDN Services: For website styling and functionality

These services have their own privacy policies, which we recommend you review.

11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside the UK for hosting and analytics purposes. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Adequacy decisions by UK authorities
  • Appropriate safeguards and contractual protections
  • Compliance with UK data protection standards

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Posting notice on our website
  • Sending email notifications for material changes
